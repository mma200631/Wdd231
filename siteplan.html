DriveNow Motors - Website plan

Site Name

DriveNow Motors

This name was chosen because it is modern, simple, and clearly communicates that the business is about buying or leasing cars. It appeals to customers who want to “drive now,” and has a professional tone suitable for an automotive dealership.

Site Purpose

The website serves as an online dealership platform where customers can browse vehicle inventory, filter by make or price, save their favorite cars, and contact the dealership. It provides an easy and responsive way for users to explore vehicles and make inquiries without needing to visit in person.

Scenerios

  • What cars do you have under $25,000?
  • Can I save my favorite vehicles to review later?
  • How do I contact a sales representative to schedule a test drive?

Color Scheme
  • Dark Blue (#002b45): Used for headings, buttons, and accent elements
  • Steel Gray (#f4f4f4): Used for background color to provide a clean and neutral base
  • Highlight Red (#d32f2f) (optional): Used for price highlights or call-to-action buttons

Typography

  • Oswald (Google Font) - Used for headings and navigation menu
  • Roboto (Google Font) - Used for body text, paragraphs, and smaller labels
